# Gordon Wintrob

Gordon Wintrob is a technology entrepreneur based in San Francisco, CA. He is the Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Newfront, a technology-enabled insurance brokerage that uses proprietary software and artificial intelligence to transform risk management. In his free time, he enjoys ultrarunning and spending time with his labradoodle Willow.

## Focus Areas
- Artificial intelligence applications in insurance and risk management
- API infrastructure and full-stack engineering
- Distributed data ingestion and workflow automation

## Experience
- **Newfront (2017–present):** Co-founder & CTO. Newfront has [raised over $300M](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/newfront-announces-200-million-investment-from-goldman-sachs-asset-management-b-capital-and-other-investors-at-a-2-2-billion-valuation-to-modernize-insurance-301523827.html) from investors including Y Combinator, Goldman Sachs, Founders Fund, and Index Ventures. The company serves thousands of clients across industries such as life sciences and construction, using AI-driven tools for contract review and coverage analysis.  
  - **Acquisition:** In December 2025, Willis Towers Watson (WTW) announced an [agreement to acquire Newfront](https://www.reuters.com/legal/transactional/willis-towers-watson-buy-brokerage-firm-newfront-13-billion-deal-2025-12-10/) in a deal valued at up to $1.3B.
- **LinkedIn (2014–2017):** Engineering Manager. Gordon led teams within LinkedIn Jobs and built distributed ingestion pipelines that aggregated millions of open roles from across the web.
- **StackLead (2014):** Co-founder. StackLead built automated research tools for sales teams. The company was part of the Y Combinator W14 batch and was acquired by LinkedIn in September 2014.
