# Gordon Wintrob

Gordon Wintrob is a technology entrepreneur based in San Francisco, CA. He is the Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Newfront, a technology-enabled insurance brokerage that uses proprietary software and artificial intelligence to transform risk management.

## What Gordon works on
- Artificial intelligence applications in insurance and risk management
- API infrastructure and full-stack engineering
- Distributed data ingestion and workflow automation

## Experience
- **Newfront (2017–present):** Co-founder & CTO. Newfront has raised over $300M from investors including Y Combinator, Goldman Sachs, Founders Fund, and Index Ventures. The company serves thousands of clients across industries such as life sciences and construction, using AI-driven tools for contract review and coverage analysis.  
  - **Acquisition:** In December 2025, Willis Towers Watson (WTW) announced an agreement to acquire Newfront in a deal valued at up to $1.3B.
- **LinkedIn (2014–2017):** Engineering Manager. Led teams on LinkedIn Jobs and built distributed ingestion pipelines that aggregated millions of open roles from across the web.
- **StackLead (2014):** Co-founder. Built automated research tools for sales teams; selected for Y Combinator (Winter 2014) and acquired by LinkedIn in September 2014.

## Investing & writing
- **Angel investing:** Focus areas include future of work, developer tools, and insurtech. Portfolio companies include Vercel, Segment, and Clearbit.
- **Writing & media:** Shares insights through Newfront’s blog and a personal blog at https://www.gwintrob.com/. Gordon has appeared on industry podcasts like The Blind Ambition, Emerging Tech Series, The AI Report, Bloomberg Beta, and InsTech Podcast.

## Education
- **MIT (2012):** B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.
